# Backers

An honest, public ledger of who funds this work. It is empty right now, and it says so in plain sight.

Everything here is built on one rule: you should never have to take my word for it. The projects hand you a proof instead of asking for your trust. This file is the same rule pointed back at the people who pay for that work to exist, a record you can read top to bottom with nothing tucked away and nothing I can quietly revise.

What funding buys is plain: time. Time to keep proving claims instead of only making them.

Recognition is kept honest. No tiers of access, no gated extras, no private thank-you notes. Just a name, in the open, in two places:

1. **Here**, in this ledger, by tier.
2. **In the release notes** for the work your support helped ship.

Both live in the repo. Both are diffable. The git history is the receipt: you can read it back and find your own entry the same way you would read back any other claim on these repos.

Sponsor through [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/EgorKhaklin). The tiers below are the ladder. The names below are whoever climbed it.
